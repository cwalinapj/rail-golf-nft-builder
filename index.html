What the NFT represents

Exclusive design-use access for the printed golf ball drop.

The minted Solana Metaplex NFT is intended to identify the owner of a unique Rail Golf ball design. Ownership gives the wallet holder exclusive use of that design when ordering these UV printed balls after launch. It does not transfer Rail Golf trademarks, protocol IP, or the Proof of Shot scanning system.

Proof of Shot seed

Four scannable QR/Data Matrix style blocks wrap the ball so identity can survive rotation, partial obstruction, and on-course camera angles.

Training loop

Rail Golf aims to turn course-play shot data into quantitative practice feedback, then reward players with crypto for verified participation and improvement.

Metaplex ready

The builder creates NFT attributes for design name, readable ID, QR requirement, colors, print method, and exclusive-use rights so a mint service can publish the asset cleanly.
